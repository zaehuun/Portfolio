EDM!

love edm & festival.

WHY?

I first encountered EDM genre when I was 12 years old. I started to like EDM because of its rhythmical feel. Since then, I have searched and listened to songs by many DJs such as Avicii, David Guetta, Steve Aoki, Hardwell, etc. After I became an adult to see them, I participated in various edm festivals, and I became more interested because I was attracted to the atmosphere of excitement and freedom.

MARSHMELLO

He is a world-famous DJ wearing a marshmallow mask. When I was a soldier, when I heard that he was coming to Korea, I paid a lot of money to go to see a performance. It is his style of music that is trendy and has melodies. Recently, I fell in love with this style and he became my favorite DJ.

It's his latest song and the song I listen to the most these days.

Marshmello & Demi Lovato - OK Not To Be OK (Duke & Jones Remix)

The Chainsmokers

When a song is released, the number one DJ is always on the list. He has long been No. 1 in DJ's annual salary and works with many pop stars. Their charm is that it's really good to listen to their old songs now. In fact, the song "Something Just Like This" is still at the top of Billboard two years after its release.

It is still a representative song that is loved.

The Chainsmokers & Coldplay - Something Just Like This

HARDWELL

He is a DJ who has been loved from the past to the present. Despite his long career, his latest songs were praised for being fresher than new DJs. He is the first DJ I became interested in and I still listen to the songs I heard then.

This is the song that I listened to the most.

Marshmello & Demi Lovato - OK Not To Be OK (Duke & Jones Remix)
